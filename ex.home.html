Frequently asked question

What services do you offer? Are your caregivers certified? Can I choose the caregiver? Is service available 24/7? What are the payment options? Do you accept insurance? What if there's an emergency? What languages do your caregivers speak? What is your cancellation policy?

What services do you offer?

We provide personal care, companionship, medication reminders, mobility assistance, meal preparation, and light housekeeping tailored to each client’s needs.

Are your caregivers certified?

Yes, all our caregivers are trained, background-checked, and certified in caregiving, CPR, and first aid.

Can I choose the caregiver?

Absolutely! We match caregivers based on personality, skills, and care needs to ensure a good fit.

Is service available 24/7?

Yes, we offer around-the-clock care including live-in and overnight services.

What are the payment options?

We accept private pay, long-term care insurance, and Medicaid waivers where applicable.

Do you accept insurance?

We accept many long-term care insurance policies. Contact us for assistance with coverage and claims.

What if there's an emergency?

We have 24/7 support and on-call staff for urgent situations. Caregivers are trained to respond and report emergencies quickly.

What languages do your caregivers speak?

We have multilingual caregivers. Let us know your language preference during consultation.

What is your cancellation policy?

We request 24 hours' notice for cancellations. For recurring care, different terms may apply.
